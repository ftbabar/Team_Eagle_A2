IT Technologies

Cloud Computing, Services and servers

What does it do?

Cloud computing is an information technology (IT) advancement and a dominant business model for IT resource distribution (Sunyaev, 2020). This is a system In which people and organisations may obtain on-demand network access to a common pool of managed and scalable IT resources such as servers, storage, and software, with cloud computing (Sunyaev, 2020). According to Gartner’s research, by 2024 the proportion of IT expenditure shifting to the cloud will increase as an after-effect of the COVID-19 pandemic. The projection is at 14.2 percent of the IT global enterprise spending market from 9.1 per cent in 2020 (Stamford, 2020).
